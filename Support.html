Support DCHS!

The Dakota County Historical Society relies on your membership and donation support in order to continue to collect,
preserve, promote and present the history of Dakota County.

Become a member or renew your membership! Members receive a subscription to Over the Years magazine,
free admission to both museums, discounts on museum store purchases and invitations to special events.

Memberships available are Student (through age 21) $25; Senior (60+) $25; Senior Household $40; Individual $40;
Household $60; Sustaining $100; Silver $250; Gold $500; and Tower $1,000.

Call 651-552-7548 for more information or to begin your membership benefits!
